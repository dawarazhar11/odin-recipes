Lahori Red Chicken Karahi

Ingredients

  • Chicken (16 pieces) 1kg
  • Lemon 2
  • Green chilies chopped 4
  • Clarified butter 1 cup
  • Green coriander chopped 1 bunch
  • Tomato chopped ½ kg
  • Black pepper crushed 1 tsp
  • Roasted white cumin 1 tsp
  • Ginger garlic paste 1 tbsp
  • Red chili powder 1 tbsp
  • Karahai masala 1 tbsp
  • Ginger chopped 2 tbsp

How to make Lahori Red Chicken Karahi

Method

  1. First wash chicken and add in wok with 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste and salt to taste till water dries.
  2. Then add ½ kg chopped tomato and 1 tbsp red chili powder and cook till tomato water dries.
  3. Now add 1 tbsp karahai masala, 1 tsp black pepper crushed, 1 tsp roasted white cumin and 1 cup oil to cook a little.
  4. In the end add 2 tbsp chopped ginger, 4 chopped green chilies, 1 chopped green coriander and 2 lemon juice to mix and serve hot.
